# Color Palette Design for Haitun English (Dolphin English)

## Introduction: Core Concept

The "Haitun English" brand should evoke feelings of intelligence, friendliness, clarity, and fluency. The primary metaphor—a dolphin navigating vast waves—is our guide. We need a palette that is professional enough to represent a learning depth but also vibrant enough to maintain user engagement.

## 1. Core Brand Colors (Primary)

*These colors form the foundation of the brand and must match the tones in your logo (image_3.png).*

| Color Name | Description & Use | Concept | Hex (approx) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Deep Dolphin Blue** | A rich, professional, and reliable dark cobalt blue. Use for the logo itself, main headers, primary navigation, and formal text. | Depth of knowledge, intelligence, reliability. | `#003B91` (or slightly deeper) |
| **Active Wave Blue** | A slightly lighter, more vibrant, friendly mid-blue. Use for main Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons, section headers, and key features. | Energy, movement, fluency. | `#2072F5` |
| **Soft Wave Blue** | A much lighter, almost aqua-blue. Use for sub-headers, background accents, and highlighting features. | Approachability, clarity. | `#7BBFFF` |

## 2. Interface Colors (Backgrounds & Text)

*These colors provide the clean canvas for content.*

| Color Name | Description & Use | Concept | Hex (approx) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Pure Surface** | A clean, light-textured off-white or very faint cream. Use as the main background for content areas. | Cleanliness, focus. | `#FCFCFA` (or `#FFFFFF`) |
| **Soft Surface** | A very pale, warm gray or off-white. Use as the background for cards, list items, or secondary content sections to create separation. | Warmth, readability. | `#F6F6F2` |
| **Ink Gray** | A very deep gray with a hint of blue. Use for primary body text and titles. Better than pure black for long reading sessions. | Legibility, professionalism. | `#2A2A2A` (with blue undertone) |
| **Storm Gray** | A medium, cool-toned gray. Use for secondary text, labels, borders, and icons. | Subtle detail. | `#757575` |

## 3. Accent & Functional Colors (Vitality & State)

*These colors add life to the interface and provide clear feedback.*

| Color Name | Description & Use | Concept | Hex (approx) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Success Teal** | A bright, clean, and energetic teal-green. Use for correct answers, progress completion, and affirmative feedback. | Clarity, success, freshness. | `#1ABC9C` |
| **Friendly Warning Orange** | A warm, soft, non-aggressive amber or warm yellow. Use for helpful reminders, suggestions, and gentle alerts. | Warmth, guidance. | `#F1C40F` (or warmer `#F39C12`) |
| **Action Purple** | A vibrant, friendly, but less formal deep purple. Use as an alternative to blue for specific modes, levels, or premium features. | Creativity, unique experience. | `#8E44AD` |

## 4. Applying the Palette: Sample UI Implementation

Let's visualize how these colors would be used in a typical app screen:

### A. Main Lesson Screen (Standard Learning Mode)

* **Background:** Pure Surface (`#FCFCFA`)
* **Header (App Bar):** Deep Dolphin Blue (`#003B91`) with text in Pure Surface.
* **Lesson Cards (Active):** Soft Surface (`#F6F6F2`) with an Active Wave Blue (`#2072F5`) top border.
* **Main Text:** Ink Gray (`#2A2A2A`).
* **Primary Lesson Button:** Active Wave Blue (`#2072F5`) with Pure Surface text.
* **Correct Answers (Feedback):** Success Teal (`#1ABC9C`) with white text.

### B. Gamified/Practice Mode (Vibrant & Energetic)

* **Background:** Soft Wave Blue (`#7BBFFF`) (or the lighter blue from the logo wave).
* **Header (App Bar):** Active Wave Blue (`#2072F5`).
* **Content Cards:** Pure Surface (`#FCFCFA`).
* **Text:** Ink Gray (`#2A2A2A`).
* **Main Challenge Button:** Friendly Warning Orange (`#F1C40F`) with Ink Gray text.
* **Bonus Text Highlights:** Action Purple (`#8E44AD`).

---

## 5. Summary of Primary Use Cases

* **Trust & Foundation (The 'Deep'):** Deep Dolphin Blue
* **Engagement & Flow (The 'Wave'):** Active Wave Blue
* **Readability & Background:** Pure Surface & Soft Surface
* **Feedback & Motivation:** Success Teal & Friendly Warning Orange

This structured approach ensures consistency, professionalism, and high readability while incorporating the brand's friendly and dynamic metaphor.
